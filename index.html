Whether you're buying your first home or upgrading to your dream villa, our residential specialists guide you through every step — from shortlisting to registration.

  • Professional home tours and virtual walkthroughs
  • Price negotiation to ensure you never overpay
  • Legal verification and title clarity guarantee

Commercial Leasing & Sales

Premium office spaces, retail showrooms, and industrial warehouses across Bengaluru's top business corridors — leased or sold at market-best rates.

  • Grade-A office spaces in IT parks and business hubs
  • Retail spaces in high-footfall commercial zones

Investment Advisory

Real estate is Bengaluru's most reliable wealth creator. Our investment advisors provide data-driven insights to maximise your returns and minimise risk.

  • Micro-market analysis and growth corridor mapping
  • ROI modelling for rental yield vs appreciation
  • Pre-launch access at developer-direct pricing
  • NRI investment advisory with repatriation guidance
  • Portfolio diversification across residential & commercial

Property Management

Own property in Bengaluru but can't manage it yourself? We handle everything — tenants, maintenance, rent collection — so your property earns while you relax.

  • Tenant screening, placement, and agreement drafting
  • Monthly rent collection and owner disbursement
  • 24/7 maintenance coordination and vendor management
  • Property inspection and condition reporting
  • Renewal negotiation and vacancy minimisation
